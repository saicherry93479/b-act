Atmanirbhar Bharat Package for MSME at a Glance

Announced by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on May 13, 2020, the Atmanirbhar Bharat package aims to support and revive MSMEs affected by the COVID-19 lockdown.

The measures include:

  • Rs 3 lakh crore of collateral-free automatic loans
  • Rs 20,000 crore subordinated debt for stressed MSMEs
  • Rs 50,000 equity infusion through a fund of funds
  • Global tender restrictions up to Rs 200 crores

New definitions classify micro, small, and medium enterprises based on investment and turnover.

Eligibility Criteria for Udyog Aadhar Registration

Any type of business entity in India can apply for Udyog Aadhar Registration, including:

Sole Proprietorship
Partnership Firm
LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)
Company
Hindu Undivided Family
Co-operative Society
Self Help Group

Eligibility is based on the maximum ceiling limit of investment made for plant & machinery (for manufacturing) or equipment (for service industry).

What is the genesis of Udyog Aadhar?+
The Udyog Aadhar was notified in September 2015 to simplify procedures for starting a business, aiming to ensure wider coverage of MSMEs to avail benefits under various schemes.
What is Udyog Aadhar Memorandum (UAM)?+
Udyog Aadhar Memorandum is the registration form where the MSME certifies its existence and provides mandatory information such as owner’s Aadhar details, bank account details, business activities, and investment in plant & machinery.
Is Aadhar OTP Verification required for Udyog Aadhar Number Registration?+
Yes, Aadhar OTP verification is required at the beginning of the registration process to validate the Aadhar Number.
Has EM-I/II been replaced by UAM?+
Yes, after the notification dated 18-09-2015, EM-I/II should be discouraged, and efforts should be made to popularize UAM.
What is the date for adopting the UAM and stopping EM-I and EM-II?+
There cannot be a different cut-off date announced for adopting UAM. However, cases of EM-I/II filing under process till 18-09-2015 may be accepted.
Whether the multiple system of registration may exist after introduction of UAM?+
All other online/offline systems of registration of MSMEs should cease to register new MSMEs forthwith. Henceforth, there should be only one system, i.e., UAM for the registration of new MSME units.
How to select NIC Code of the Activity performed by the enterprise?+
NIC code can be selected once the Aadhar number is validated and major activity (manufacturing or service) is selected. A three-stage drop-down list is available to simplify the selection.
Is there a provision to verify the UAM credentials by the procurement agencies including government departments and PSUs?+
Yes, NIC has been advised to provide a way for various e-procurement portals to benefit from the UAM portal for online verification of a MSME unit.
Is there a difference in the information sought under EM-I/II and the new UAM?+
Yes, the UAM seeks lesser information than EM-I/II to promote ease of doing business for MSMEs.

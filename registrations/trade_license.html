What is a Trade License?

A Trade License is a legal document that permits individuals or companies to conduct business in a specific area.

Documents Required

What is Shops & Establishments Act?

What is Shops & Establishments Act? Shops And Establishment Act was enacted to regulate the working hours, weekly/monthly off, payment of wages, terms and condition of employment, holidays, leave etc. of persons employed in shops or any commercial establishments. This Act is applicable nationwide to shops, commercial establishments, hotels, restaurants, eating houses, theatres and other places of public amusement or entertainments. Every state has his Own Shops And Establishment Act which is more or less same with other states and very less difference or any changes. How much time taken to get the Shop and Establishment license issued?+
Normally 15-20 days if physical submission and/or departmental inspection of premises is essential. In some states, it can take 1-3 days in case of a fully online system.
What is the eligibility for getting a trade license for an owner?+
There are two major prerequisites: the person must have attained the age of 18 years and must not have any criminal records in their name.
Can I run my shop without a trade license under the Shop and Establishment Act?+
Running a shop or commercial establishment without obtaining a trade license is illegal as per the Act. The concerned authority may seize or lock your trade business with or without prior intimation.
Is the license once issued can be revoked/suspended?+
If the licensee fails to comply with any of the conditions of the license, the authority of the concerned zone or any other authorized officer may revoke or suspend the license after giving notice.
Whether license can be denied/cancelled?+
A license can be denied/cancelled under the following conditions:
  1. In case there is encroachment on government or public land, it shall be ascertained if it is protected under special laws. If protected, the application shall be processed for grant of a Limited License only for the period of such protection and shall stand null and void thereafter.
  2. The licensee cannot claim/demand immunity from action or penalties on the ground that the Trade License has been granted without verification of such applicable provisions of encroachments.
  3. License shall be denied/cancelled in case it is found that there is encroachment on right of way or any other encroachments that are not protected under special laws.
  4. License is liable to be cancelled if any trade activity is noticed outside the establishment such as storage/display of wares for sale/placement of display board/name board for occupation of the space outside the establishment in any other manner.
  5. Any existing valid license shall be deemed to be cancelled in case of change of ownership/change of address/change of nature of business/change in name of the company or death of the owner.

Can I update my license details?+
Yes, you can file an update with the Authority for any changes in management, ownership, business particulars, etc.

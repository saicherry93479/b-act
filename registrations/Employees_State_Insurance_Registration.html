Mandatory for companies with 10/20 or more employees, Salary up to Rs 21,000 PM

ESI Scheme Overview

The ESI Scheme is governed by the Employees' State Insurance Corporation, implemented as per the ESI Act 1948. It applies to factories and establishments with 10 or more workers (20 or more in some states) drawing a salary of up to Rs. 21,000 per month.

Employer contributes 4.75% of the wages, while employees contribute 1.75%. Employees earning less than Rs. 137 per day are exempt from their contribution.

Legal Requirements for Employers

Comply with rules and take registration within 15 days of applicability.
Enroll all eligible employees and provide them with a Temporary Identity Certificate (TIC).
File monthly returns online with details of employees and contributions.
Maintain records such as wage records, accident registers, and inspection books.
File Monthly Return Every month Employer file ESI return online with details of Employee, salary and ESI contribution amount within the due date.
Remittance of Monthly ESI The total amount of contribution (Employer and Employee) is to be deposited in any branch of SBI by ONLINE generation of a challan through ESIC portal using his credentials. ESIC has facilitated payment of ESI Contribution online by employer via the payment gateway of 58 banks in addition to SBI.
File Half Yearly Return File half yearly return and report to ESIC authorities if there is any change in business activity, address, ownership and the management, maintenance of registers and records etc.

What do you mean by salary for this ESI purpose?+
Salary or wages is Basic Pay plus Dearness Allowance (DA) plus Retaining Allowance.
Do I need to be physically present for the process?+
No, you don't need to be physically present for the process. The process is 100% online.
What if ESI registration is not done?+
As per the rule, within 15 days of ESI Scheme applicability, it is the legal obligation of the employer to register with ESIC and get insurance numbers for all employees drawing a monthly salary up to Rs 21,000.
Is Permanent Account Number compulsory for Registration of ESI?+
Yes, a Permanent Account Number (PAN) is mandatory for ESI registration.
Is Digital Signature of Employer mandatory in ESI Registration?+
Yes, a Class 2 Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is required for ESI registration and to file all returns.
How is ESI deducted from salary?+
For ESI deduction, the maximum limit of salary of the employee is Rs 21,000 per month. The employer is liable to contribute only on Rs 21,000, which amounts to Rs 998.
What is the time limit for Payment of ESI Contribution?+
The payment must be made within 21 days of the last day of the month in which the contribution is due.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is One Person Company? +

An OPC is a type of private limited company with a single shareholder and director, introduced to help solo entrepreneurs.

What are the Advantages / Features of the OPC? +

Limited liability, single ownership, and fewer compliance requirements make OPCs advantageous for small businesses.

What are the documents required for Company Registration? +

Documents like PAN, Aadhar, address proof, and ID proof are needed for registration.

What Type of Business Entities are in India? +

In India, popular entities include sole proprietorship, partnership, LLP, private limited, and public limited companies.

What are the Minimum Requirement of Company Registration? +

Minimum requirements include a unique name, a registered office, and one director/shareholder for OPC.

Is there any threshold limits for an OPC to mandatory get converted? +

Yes, OPC must convert to a private or public limited company if its turnover exceeds certain limits.

Are there any hidden charges after I take any of your service? +

No hidden charges are applied; all fees are transparently communicated upfront.

Can an existing Company be converted to other form of other type of Company? +

Yes, companies can be converted to other forms, such as from LLP to private limited or vice versa.

Can a salaried person become director of company? +

Yes, a salaried individual can become a director, but they may need employer permission if employed elsewhere.

Can I Change address of company after I take a commercial space? +

Yes, the company address can be changed with the necessary filings to the ROC.

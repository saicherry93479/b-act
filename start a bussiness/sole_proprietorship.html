When is a Shops and Establishments Act license required? +
Every shop and establishment needs to register itself mandatorily under the Telangana Shops and Establishment Act, 1988 within 30 days of commencement of work.
What is the objective of the Shops and Establishments Act? +
To secure uniform benefits for employees working in different establishments, from shops, commercial establishments and residential hotels to restaurants, theatres and other places of public amusement or entertainment.
How much time required for obtaining the Shop Act Registration Certificate? +
Obtaining of Shop Act usually takes about 1-2 working days subject to the receipt of supporting documents from the client and the approval of the Govt. Authority.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is One Person Company? +
A One Person Company (OPC) is a business structure in India where a single individual can form and operate a company. It provides legal recognition and limited liability protection to the sole shareholder.
What are the Advantages / features of the OPC? +
The key advantages of an OPC include:
  • Easy to set up and operate
  • Limited liability for the sole shareholder
  • Separate legal entity with its own PAN and bank account
  • Can avail tax benefits and incentives
  • Easier to maintain accounts and compliance
What are the documents required for Company Registration? +
The required documents for company registration include:
  • PAN cards of the shareholder and director
  • Passport photos of the shareholder and director
  • Aadhaar cards of the shareholder and director
  • Address proof of the shareholder and director
  • Partnership deed (for partnerships)
What Type of Business Entities are in India? +
The main types of business entities in India include:
  • Sole Proprietorship
  • Partnership
  • Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)
  • Private Limited Company
  • One Person Company (OPC)
  • Public Limited Company
What are the Minimum Requirement of Company Registration? +
The minimum requirements for company registration in India include:
  • Minimum of one shareholder and one director
  • Minimum paid-up capital of Rs. 1 lakh
  • Registered office address in India
  • Unique Company Identification Number (CIN)
Is there any threshold limits for an OPC to mandatory get converted? +
Yes, there are certain threshold limits for an OPC to mandatorily get converted to a private limited company:
  • Paid-up capital exceeds Rs. 50 lakhs
  • Average annual turnover for the preceding 3 financial years exceeds Rs. 2 crores

Can an existing Company be converted to other form of other type of Company? +
Yes, an existing company can be converted to a different type of company structure, such as:
  • Conversion of a private limited company to a public limited company
  • Conversion of a partnership firm to a private limited company
  • Conversion of a sole proprietorship to a private limited company
The specific process and requirements depend on the type of conversion.
Can a salaried person become director of company? +
Yes, a salaried person can become a director of a company. The person must meet the eligibility criteria set by the Companies Act, such as being of sound mind, not disqualified, and in compliance with the company's articles of association.
Can I Change address of company after I take a commercial space? +
Yes, you can change the registered address of your company after taking a commercial space. The process involves filing the necessary forms with the Registrar of Companies and updating the company's records.

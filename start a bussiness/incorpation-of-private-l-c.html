Incorporation of Private Limited Company in India

Ideal for businesses looking to expand and raise capital.

Characteristics of a Private Limited Company

Limited liability for directors and shareholders.
Minimum 2 directors, maximum 15 directors, and 200 shareholders.
Can raise capital through the sale of shares.
Directors have limited liability; perpetual succession.

Documents Required for Company Incorporation

PAN Card Copy of Proposed Directors & Shareholders
Passport Size Photograph of Directors
Aadhar Card Copy of Directors
Address Proof (Bank Statement / Utility Bill)
Rent Agreement Copy (if applicable)
NOC from Property Owner

Company Registration Procedure in India

Step 1: Documentation
Step 2: Filing Application
Step 3: Issuance of Certificate
Step 4: Post-Incorporation Compliance

Pre-requisite for Company Incorporation

File Commencement of Business (COB) e-form INC 20A within 180 days.
Appointment of a Statutory Auditor.
Filing of ROC Annual Forms.
Filing of Director e-KYC.

Additional expenses for audit and ROC filings if a Startup Company: approx. Rs 40-50K annually.

Post-Incorporation Compliance Requirements

Proposed Company name must be unique.
All Proposed Directors must have Active DIN.
Digital Signature for Directors/Shareholders.
Decide Paid-up and Authorized Share Capital.
Select Company Registration State and Office Address.
Decide Bank Authorized Signatory and Bank for Account Setup.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Private Limited Company?+
A Private Limited Company is a business entity offering limited liability and up to 200 shareholders, where the ownership is privately held.
What are the main business entities in India?+
The main types of business entities in India are Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), Private Limited Company, and Public Limited Company.
How much time is required to register a Private Limited Company?+
The entire process to register a Private Limited Company in India generally takes around 10-15 days, depending on the processing time of the Registrar of Companies (ROC) and the completion of documentation.
What is the minimum number of directors required for a Private Limited Company?+
A minimum of two directors is required to incorporate a Private Limited Company in India. However, the maximum number allowed is fifteen directors.
Is it necessary to be physically present for company registration?+
No, physical presence is not required for the company registration process as it can be done online. All documents can be submitted electronically.
Do I need to have an office space to register the company?+
Yes, a registered office address within India is required to register a Private Limited Company. This can be either a residential or commercial address.
Can a Private Limited Company be converted into another type of company?+
Yes, a Private Limited Company can be converted into a Public Limited Company, LLP, or other entities by following the prescribed legal process and compliance requirements.
What are the post-registration compliances for a Private Limited Company?+
After registration, a Private Limited Company must fulfill various compliances, including filing annual returns, holding board meetings, and maintaining statutory records.
What is the cost of registering a Private Limited Company?+
The cost of registering a Private Limited Company varies depending on authorized capital, state fees, and professional fees. On average, the cost ranges from Rs. 7,000 to Rs. 15,000.
